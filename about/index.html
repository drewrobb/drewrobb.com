Drew Robb

I’m currently a Infrastructure Engineer at Strava. My work involves many interesting projects mostly using Scala.

Previously I was Director of Data Systems at SocialFlow. My work there was generally in python. Some of the projects I worked on included a distributed counting and analytics framework using Redis/Cassandra, a link shortener.

I run archlinux on all of my personal machines. My environment consists of xmonad, zsh, emacs. You can look at my poorly maintained dotfiles repo.

I grew up on Oahu. I studied Physics, Math, and Computer Science at Harvard. I Kitesurf, Run, Bike, Sail. I actively play Go and I’m about 1 Dan in rank.
